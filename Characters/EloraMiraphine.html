Elora Miraphine

Elora Miraphine was born beneath the golden canopies of Larethian Grove, a place where sunlight danced on the leaves and life flourished at her touch.

With her soothing voice and gentle hands, Elora nurtured both the land and its creatures, embodying the essence of life itself.

When nature began to wither and the world’s balance faltered, Elora sought the heart of the forest, merging her spirit with the earth’s pulse.

On the seventh day, she emerged as a goddess, her presence now a radiant force that breathed life into all she touched.

Known as the Verdant Flame, Elora’s beauty is matched only by her power. She is both a protector and a seductress, coaxing life to bloom while holding the fierce strength of nature within.
