Davric Varkane

Born in the shadowed halls of Valdaran’s high temple, Davric Varkane was raised in doctrine and steel.

As a young priest, he showed unmatched zeal, rooting out heresies with chilling precision and unwavering faith.

His rise was swift—by thirty, he wore the mantle of Inquisitor, feared as much for his wisdom as his interrogations.

When whispers of forbidden magic stirred within the empire, Davric led the purge, unearthing truths others dared not speak.

Behind his cold eyes lies a storm of conviction; to him, mercy is a luxury, but justice is sacred.

He walks with the authority of gods, wielding divine fire not just to heal—but to cleanse.
