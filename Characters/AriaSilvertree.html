Aria Silvertree

Aria Silvertree was born beneath the luminous canopies of the Larethian Grove.

Though slender and soft-spoken, Aria quickly proved herself a fierce and agile combatant.

One fateful spring, a strange sickness began to seep into the roots of the forest.

She now serves in the elven ranks, driven by her unwavering honor.

She is thoughtful and deeply idealistic.
