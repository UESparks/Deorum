Neida Dravenhart

Neida Dravenhart was born beneath the shadow of Ashspire, a volcanic peak whispered to house the last dragons of the old world.

As a child, she wandered too far and vanished into the mountains—returning days later with seared skin, dragon-blooded eyes, and a name no longer forgotten.

She trained as a blademaster, wielding flame as both weapon and will, and hunted the very beasts whose fire had once nearly ended her.

But when the Dragon King awakened and scorched the southern realms, it was Neida who challenged him in the sky—and fell with him into the sea of flame.

She rose again in firelight, no longer mortal, her soul fused with dragonkind and crowned in ash and gold.

Now called the Flame God, Neida Dravenhart reigns from the Ember Seat, feared by tyrants and worshiped by those who warm their hands beside her fire.
